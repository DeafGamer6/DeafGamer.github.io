About me

I am a PhD Student at the School of Computer Science and Informatics at Cardiff University.

My work is in the HCI (Human Computer Interaction) domain with a focus on Audio Accessibility.

My PhD entitled "Deaf Play: A Narrative Thesis on the social play contexts of Deaf players" is still in progress. Though will be available here upon completion.

If you are interested in my research, even if it is not available here, feel free to contact me!

Areas of research interest

:

  • Audio Accessibility
  • d/DHH Community
  • Accessibility in Video games and other interactive Medias
  • Social interaction through games
